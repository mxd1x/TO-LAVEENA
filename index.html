TO A
PRINCESS

I find myself compelled to send you these words, not with the artistry of grand declarations, but with the simplicity of a heart that beats in rhythm with yours.

In a world where words often flutter like delicate butterflies, my intention is pure and straightforward. I wish to be the constant in your life, a haven where you can be unapologetically yourself.

Your uniqueness is a melody that resonates in my soul. I long to be the one who listens to the cadence of your heart, understanding the nuances of your thoughts without the need for spoken words.

Should life cast shadows upon your journey, I want to be the one standing beside you, a source of support and understanding. Your joy is my joy, and your burdens are mine to share.

Your absence leaves a quiet ache, and I eagerly await the day when your laughter fills the spaces between us once again. In a whimsical dream, I'd choose the ability to read minds, not for control, but to know the beauty that resides in your thoughts.

May the universe conspire to bring you healing swiftly, so your light can shine brightly again. Until then, I patiently anticipate the moment our paths converge anew.

With admiration,
Dave
